## 🏳️‍🌈 Fighting for Pride – The Italian Path to LGBTQIA+ Resistance

**Fighting for Pride** is a hybrid exhibition dedicated to the history of the Italian Pride and LGBTQIA+ rights activism. The project revolves around the documentation Center “Flavia Madaschi”, the biggest community archive in Italy and Europe specialized in the preservation of LGBTQIA+ history. 
Its main objective is to show the archive and its contents to a wider public, while also educating about the often overlooked Italian Pride history.

## 📖 Overview

The exhibition combines both physical and digital layers, so that the it can be experienced differently by everyone.
On the physical layer, the visitor follows a path resembling a Pride march, while observing displayed items selected from the Flavia Madaschi Center.
On the digital level, the visitor scans the QR codes scattered throughout the exhibition; this will lead to a companion WebApp in which additional material is stored.
Another interaction on the digital level is the VR experience, which starts once the visitor reaches the last hall at the end of the exhibition.

## ✨ Features

- 🖼️ A 3D model of the physical exhibition created with Blender
- 📚 A companion WebApp prototyped with Figma
- 🎮 A Twine narrative experience
